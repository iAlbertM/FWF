Description

The Space

Welcome to Casa de Crystal. One of the most significant architectural houses in the Caribbean. Located on the magical, tropical island of Vieques, just off the coast of Puerto Rico, this exclusive private residence is the ultimate place to smooth the edges of one's soul. Casa de Crystal is situated on the hillside in the exclusive Puerto Real area of Vieques and is surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens and tropical forests. Casa de Crystal boasts 20ft glass walls that allow for one of the most beautiful views of the Caribbean, the islands of Esperanza and the glorious sunsets of Vieques.

Designed by architect Henry Myerberg, Casa de Crystal is a sculptural masterpiece that has been featured on the cover of Metropolitan Home and in several travel magazines including Travel & Leisure, Conde Naste Traveler and Tropical Modern. The two bedroom, four bath home also has a private pool, personal gym, Buddha meditation pavilion and several private outdoor spaces for entertaining or romantic dinners. Whatever your needs are, Casa de Crystal offers a unique and truly magical experience for your mind, body and soul.
